# Stage 1 - Brand Materials

Goal: turn vague self-introduction into website-ready brand material.

Ask for or extract:

1. Brand name or remembered name
2. Audience
3. Audience problem
4. Offer
5. Point of view
6. Proof and experience
7. Tone and personality
8. Links and primary CTA
9. Hero copy candidates
10. Navigation tab candidates

## Required Output

```text
# Brand Materials

## One-Line Identity
## Audience
## Problem They Have
## Offer
## Point of View
## Proof
## Tone
## Links
## Hero Copy Candidates
## Navigation Tabs
## Missing Material
```

## Rules

- If proof is weak, say what is missing instead of exaggerating.
- If the student has SNS posts, reviews, CSV files, lectures, products, or projects, summarize only evidence-backed items.
- The one-line identity should feel spoken and memorable, not corporate.
